## 🎯 Pro Player Map Tips

1. **Write down the map** as you explore
2. **Note item locations** for return trips
3. **Mark danger zones** (Dungeon, Bridge, Peak)
4. **Mark safe zones** (Forest, Village, River)
5. **Plan your route** before moving
6. **Always use `look`** in new locations
7. **Check `inventory`** frequently
8. **Track `stats`** for alignment/health
